About Me.

I live on Lookout Mountain with my husband Aaron and our cat Tuxedo. I enjoy watching Jeopardy!, having wine with my gal pals, and laughing with my family and friends. I think the best part of waking up is truly Folgers in my cup. I'm not a snob about coffee. I'm a snob without it.

Aside from winning a Computer Award in the 8th grade for making a pretty sweet Powerpoint presentation, I don't have much experience with coding/ technology in general. I am hoping I didn't peak as I am excited to dig into the next 10 weeks with Coding Temple and come out the other side with the tools to make an even sweeter Powerpoint.

Why Coding Temple?

I took an interest in coding after seeing a few of my friends in college take coding courses. My skills and interests seem to align. I enjoy problem solving, organizing, and detail oriented work. I love seeing a project come together especially after some sweat and tears. I enjoy being challenged and competing with myself which is something I am eager to experience during the next 10 weeks and hopefully after graduating and entering the professional world.
